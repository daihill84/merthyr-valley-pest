Audit-ready rodent control for Welsh farms

These days farm audits aren't just about chucking a bit of bait down somewhere and hoping for the best. The inspectors want to see proper documented Integrated Pest Management (IPM), clear monitoring records, proofing work, and proof you're actually keeping on top of rodents.

Everything we do is built around Red Tractor, SALSA, and the big retailer standards. We set up mapped monitoring points, carry out regular checks, track any activity trends, and give you straightforward written advice to cut future risks.

Wherever we can, we go for physical proofing and proper control instead of just leaving bait stations everywhere forever. It cuts down on secondary poisoning risks to your livestock, and it looks a lot better when the auditor turns up.
