Protecting Your Infrastructure & Yield

Solar farms are high-value targets for rodents and burrowing animals. From chewed DC cabling to ground-subsidence caused by badger or rabbit activity, the financial risk is significant. David (Ex-Pestokil) provides specialized Asset Protection Contracts designed for O&M (Operation & Maintenance) providers.

Rodent Wire-Damage Prevention

Rats and mice have an affinity for cable insulation. A single gnawed wire can cause string failure or, in extreme cases, electrical fires. We maintain high-frequency perimeter monitoring to stop rodents before they reach the arrays.

Burrowing & Ground Stability

Rabbits and Moles can compromise the stability of mounting structures and inverter pads. We provide professional mole catching and humane rabbit management to ensure the structural integrity of the site.

Inverter & Substation Security

Inverters generate heat, making them a primary nesting site for rodents. We specialize in non-toxic proofing for cabinet entries and substations, ensuring your critical equipment remains offline-risk free.

O&M Compliant Monitoring & Reporting

We provide full digital audit trails for every site visit, satisfying insurance requirements and asset owner standards. 24/7 emergency response for suspected cable damage incidents.
