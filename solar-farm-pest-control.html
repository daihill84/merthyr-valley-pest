Protecting Your Infrastructure & Yield

Solar farms are high-value targets for rodents and burrowing animals. From chewed DC cabling to ground-subsidence caused by badger or rabbit activity, the financial risk is significant. David (Ex-Pestokil) provides specialized Asset Protection Contracts designed for O&M (Operation & Maintenance) providers.

Rodent Wire-Damage Prevention

Rats and mice have an affinity for cable insulation. A single gnawed wire can cause string failure or electrical fires. We maintain high-frequency perimeter monitoring to stop rodents before they reach the arrays.

Rabbit & Ground Stability

Rabbit populations can compromise the stability of mounting structures and inverter pads through extensive burrowing. We provide humane, compliant population management to protect site integrity.
